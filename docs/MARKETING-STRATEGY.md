# Zenti — Creative Marketing & Growth Strategy (2026-06-14)

> Lens: gstack `plan-ceo-review` / `office-hours` (narrowest wedge, 10-star product, real demand) +
> `ui-ux-pro-max` landing/conversion patterns. Grounded in the Kenyan market (M-Pesa dominance,
> WhatsApp ubiquity, chama culture, eTIMS, rental MRI tax).

## THE BIG INSIGHT — "Collectors are the growth engine"
Don't market to "everyone in Kenya." Win the **person who collects money from many people**, because
onboarding ONE collector pulls 10–50 payers onto Zenti — who then use it for everything else.

**Collectors:** landlords, chama treasurers, church/harambee organizers, school bursars, SACCOs,
sports/estate clubs, matatu saccos, event organizers. This is **B2B2C virality**: one acquisition → a cluster.

## FLAGSHIP WEDGE — Rent collection (the goldmine)
**Landlord dashboard:** units → tenants → line items (rent / water / maintenance) → **"Collect"** →
fires WhatsApp + in-app requests → tenants pay via Zenti → auto-reconciled records + receipts.

Why it wins:
- **Recurring + high-intent + painful today** (month-end shop queues, M-Pesa reconciliation chaos, manual books).
- Solves BOTH sides: landlord gets clean records & arrears tracking; tenant gets one-tap pay + receipt.
- Every building = a monthly cluster of new Zenti users.
- **Tax hook:** rental income is taxable (KRA MRI 7.5% on gross rent) → landlords need records → Zenti Business + eTIMS.

10-star version: auto-reminders, arrears/aging, statements, receipts, partial payments, deposit tracking,
multi-property, "send to all" one tap, MRI/eTIMS-ready exports.

## DISTRIBUTION RAIL — WhatsApp-native
Kenya runs on WhatsApp. Every request/money-link rides WhatsApp; the **web claim page converts payers → users**.
- WhatsApp Business API for collection notifications & reminders.
- Viral loop: collect → WhatsApp message → claim page → "Download Zenti" → new user → new collector someday.
- "Powered by Zenti" on every link, receipt, and statement.

## VERTICAL PLAYBOOKS (same collector pattern, copy-paste GTM)
1. **Chamas** (group savings — millions of Kenyans) → Pools; treasurer collects monthly. Huge.
2. **Harambee / church / fundraisers** → shareable public progress pages (organic reach built in).
3. **School fees** → bursars / parent groups.
4. **Estates, SACCOs, clubs, matatu saccos** → dues collection.
Each = one collector → many payers → retention via everyday use.

## VIRAL / REFERRAL MECHANICS
- Claim page always upsells install + **founding-user perks** (early card access, fee-free period).
- Referral: inviter + invitee both get a perk (or **card-waitlist priority** — tie into the 4th unrevealed design).
- **Public pool/harambee pages** are inherently shareable (progress bar = social proof + reach).
- Scarcity engine: the Card waitlist (4 designs, one unrevealed) drives hype.

## METRICS THAT MATTER (not vanity)

repeat collection rate · monthly collected volume. (Avoid fake "users" counts on the site.)
