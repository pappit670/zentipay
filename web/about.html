About

Money should feel
like it’s on your side.

Zenti is a small team in Kenya building a calmer way to pay and get paid. We’re early and still in testing, so this page is about what we believe and what we’re making, not big numbers we don’t have yet.

Why Zenti exists

Paying for things in Kenya works, but it asks a lot of you. You squint at a till number on a faded sticker, type it carefully, then type it again because you weren’t sure. You send a screenshot to prove you paid. You scroll through a pile of M-Pesa messages trying to remember who got what and when. None of it is hard on its own. Together, it’s a lot of small friction that adds up across a day.

We started Zenti because the people doing this every day deserve better tools. The mama mboga reconciling the day’s sales. The chama treasurer chasing contributions. The person who just wants to split a bill without a back-and-forth. We think paying should be quick and clear, and that you should always be able to see where your money went.

What we believe

Clarity over complexity

A payment should be obvious: who, how much, and did it go through. We’d rather cut a feature than add one more thing to read on the screen.

Keep what already works

M-Pesa, paybills, till numbers — people already trust these. Zenti sits alongside the tools you use, instead of asking you to throw them away.

Honest pricing

Whatever it costs, you’ll see it before you pay. No surprise fees buried in the small print, and no charges we can’t explain in plain words.

Built for Kenya first

We’re not adapting something made for somewhere else. Zenti is built around how money actually moves here, from sending a few bob to a friend to keeping records clean enough for KRA. That means working well on the phones people actually carry, on the networks they actually have, in the way they already pay. Kenya first, and properly, before anywhere else.

What we’re making

Two products, one idea — make paying feel simple on both sides of the counter.

Zenti Pay is for people: pay anyone, get paid from a link, save toward a goal, and keep your money in one clear place. See the features.

Zenti Business is for merchants: take payments without the screenshot dance, and see your sales add up cleanly at the end of the day. Read about Zenti Business.

Say hello

We read everything. If you’ve got an idea, a frustration, or you just want to know what we’re up to, write to us at hello@zentipay.app. We’d genuinely like to hear from you.

— The Zenti team, Nairobi
