What you need to open an account

Opening a Zenti Pay account takes a few minutes in the app. You’ll need:

  • A Kenyan phone number, so we can verify it’s really you
  • A valid ID for KYC — your Kenyan national ID or passport
  • A few minutes to confirm your details

KYC is short for “know your customer”. It’s the basic identity check every regulated money service in Kenya has to do. It keeps your account yours and keeps everyone safer.

Managing your account

Once you’re in, everything lives in the app:

  • Update your details when your phone number, name or email changes
  • Freeze your card straight away if you misplace it, then unfreeze when it turns up
  • See every transaction in one clear list, not scattered across messages
  • Get statements when you need a record for yourself or for KRA

Closing your account and your data

It’s your account, so you can close it any time from the app. We’ll help you move out any remaining balance first. For how we store and protect your information — and your rights under Kenya’s Data Protection Act, 2019 — see our security and policies page.

Need help?

If something doesn’t look right, or you just have a question, write to us at support@zentipay.app and a person will get back to you.
