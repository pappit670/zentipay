FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

+ Is Zenti free?
Starter is free forever — get paid, see your revenue, and keep records. Pro and Max add invoicing, reconciliation, team accounts, and deep compliance for growing businesses.
+ Do I need a KRA PIN to start?
No. Start with just your business name and phone. Add your KRA PIN later, when you’re ready for eTIMS and tax features.
+ How much is the business QR?
A one-time KES 100 activation. We validate your details, then your QR goes live. It’s the same on every plan.
+ Does Zenti work with M-Pesa?
Yes. Connect your till or Paybill and payments import automatically — sorted and reconciled for you.
+ What is eTIMS, and why does it matter?
Since 2026, KRA requires electronic tax invoices. Zenti makes every sale eTIMS-ready, so you stay compliant without the month-end scramble.
+ What kinds of businesses use Zenti?
One adaptable rail for landlords, chamas, shops, salons, schools, freelancers, restaurants, and more — your labels, same engine.
+ Is my data safe?
Your data is private and encrypted, and you stay in control of it. We never sell it.

Zenti’s role is to provide clarity, records, automation, and financial infrastructure. Zenti does not present itself as a law firm, accounting firm, tax advisor, or financial advisor. The platform’s purpose is to help users move money, understand their business, maintain records, and simplify operations.
