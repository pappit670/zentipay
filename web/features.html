01 — Pay anyone

Money should move
as fast as a word.

Where it came from

Paying someone in Kenya means remembering till numbers, paybills, account numbers — then double-checking you typed them right. One wrong digit and the money’s gone to a stranger.

How it works

Type an amount, pick a person, swipe. Zenti routes it over M-Pesa, bank, or balance — whichever fits — and confirms it landed. No copying numbers between apps.

✓ It clears in seconds, to the right person, every time.

02 — The Zen Tag

Your tag is
the new number.

Where it came from

A phone number is fragile. It’s tied to a SIM, it changes, it gets lost or swapped. And handing out digits to get paid has always felt clumsy. Your identity on Zenti shouldn’t depend on a line.

How it works

You get a Zen Tag — a handle like @wanjiku. Share it instead of your number. Anyone can search a name or tag and find you, then pay or request in a tap. It’s also a real tappable tag for phone-to-phone pay, so the tag is how Zenti knows it’s you.

For creators & groups

Turn your Zen Tag into a promo code. Partner with brands or your community, share your tag as the code, and earn from what it brings in — one handle for your audience, your payments, and your partnerships.

✓ One handle. Findable, payable, yours — even if your line isn’t.

03 — Quick pay

Paid in under
five seconds.

Where it came from

At a till or a stall, the slow part isn’t the money — it’s the menus. Opening an app, finding the right screen, tapping through. Paying should be the quickest thing you do all day.

How it works

Quick Access drops you straight onto the Pay screen the moment you open Zenti. Type the amount, double-tap to confirm with Face ID, done. Open, amount, confirm — three taps, no hunting.

✓ The fastest screen you’ll touch all day.

04 — Money links

Send a link.
Get paid back.

Where it came from

“Send me your number, I’ll send it… did you get it? Screenshot?” The whole back-and-forth of getting paid by a friend is a small daily tax on everyone.

How it works

Create a link for any amount and drop it in WhatsApp or a message. They open it, pay with M-Pesa or their balance, and you’re notified the moment it lands — even if they’ve never used Zenti.

✓ No numbers shared, no screenshots chased.

05 — Split the bill

Split it.
Skip the maths.

Where it came from

One person pays for the table, the trip, the group gift — then spends a week doing mental arithmetic and nudging people for their share. Money between friends shouldn’t strain the friendship.

How it works

Split any amount across people in a tap — evenly or by custom shares. Everyone gets a request to their Zen Tag, pays their part, and you watch who’s settled in real time. No spreadsheet, no chasing.

✓ Everyone pays their share, you keep the friendship.

06 — Tap to pay

Two phones touch.
It’s done.

Where it came from

At a stall or between friends, the slowest part of paying is finding the right merchant or typing a till. The amount is known — only the “who” is fiddly.

How it works

Enter the amount and hold your phone to a Zen Tag or another phone. NFC identifies the recipient instantly, you confirm with Face ID or fingerprint, and it’s paid. Input. Tap. Pay.

✓ No till entry. No wrong-recipient errors.

07 — Scan & go

Point, confirm,
walk on.

Where it came from

Queues build when paying takes too long. A scan should be the fastest thing you do all day, not a hunt through menus.

How it works

Open the camera, scan any Zenti QR, check the amount, confirm. The receipt shows on both screens at once. (A merchant QR costs a one-time KES 100 to set up — we’re upfront about that.)

✓ Paid before the cashier looks up.

09 — Pools

Save together,
see everything.

Where it came from

How it works

Create a pool, invite people, and everyone sees who paid, how much, and what’s left — live. Contributions, the running total, and withdrawals are all on the same screen.
