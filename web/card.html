The card

A card that finally fits
how Kenya pays.

We wanted a card you’d actually reach for — not one that sits in a drawer because half the shops “hawakubali card.” So we built one that works everywhere Zenti does.

No more squinting at a till sticker. No “weka kwa till namba 832…”, no wrong paybill, no wondering if it went through. The Zen Card lives in your phone — you tap, it’s paid, you both feel the tick.

Pay the supermarket. Pay your landlord. Pay mama mboga at the stall. Same card, same tap. Truth is, kwa ground ni cash na M-Pesa — cards never really caught on here. We’re changing that, because the Zen Card meets both.

Set up in seconds. Right in the app.

The Zen Card lives inside Zenti. To get started, open the app, tap the plus, and it’s yours — no forms, no branch, no waiting for plastic in the post. When you want to pay, just double-tap, tap the till, and you’re set. Your Daily Cash and partner discounts still count on every purchase, so you never miss out on a saving.
